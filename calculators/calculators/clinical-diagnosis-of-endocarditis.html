Modified Duke Criteria (2000)

Diagnosis of Endocarditis:

  • Definite Endocarditis: 2 major, 1 major + 3 minor, or 5 minor criteria
  • Possible Endocarditis: 1 major + 1 minor, or 3 minor criteria
  • Rejected: <0.5 probability of endocarditis (strict criteria not met)

Clinical Notes:

  • Blood cultures should be obtained before antibiotics when possible
  • Echocardiography (TTE then TEE if TTE negative) is essential
  • Prosthetic valve endocarditis has modified criteria
  • CIED (cardiac implantable electronic device) infections have specific criteria

Maintenance Fluid Calculation

Hourly Requirements (Simplified 4-2-1 Method)
Instructions: Enter the child's weight in kilograms. Choose between the 4-2-1 rule (simplified) or Holliday-Segar (detailed calculation).
4-2-1 Rule (Simplified):
  • First 10 kg: 4 mL/kg/hr
  • Next 10 kg: 2 mL/kg/hr
  • Each kg above 20 kg: 1 mL/kg/hr
Range: 1-100 kg

Formula & References
Scoring System:
  • C: Congestive heart failure = 1 point
  • H: Hypertension = 1 point
  • A₁: Age ≥75 = 2 points; 65-74 = 1 point
  • D: Diabetes = 1 point
  • S₁: Prior stroke/TIA/thromboembolism = 2 points
  • V: Vascular disease = 1 point
  • A₂: Age 65-74 or Female sex = 1 point
  • Sc: Age ≥65 with hypertension, diabetes, or female = 1 point

Clinical Note: This calculator aids in shared decision-making. Consider individual patient factors, bleeding risk (HAS-BLED), preferences, and drug interactions when recommending anticoagulation.

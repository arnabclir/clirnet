Clinical Reference & Interpretation
Normal Range: 0-400 cells/μL (0-0.4 K/μL)
Mild Eosinophilia: 400-1,500 cells/μL
Moderate Eosinophilia: 1,500-5,000 cells/μL
Severe Eosinophilia: >5,000 cells/μL
Clinical Significance: Elevated eosinophils may indicate parasitic infections, allergic reactions, asthma, drug reactions, lymphoproliferative disorders, or hypereosinophilic syndrome.
Sources: Wallach's Interpretation of Diagnostic Tests; Mayo Clinic Laboratories Reference Values

Braden Score Information

Total Score Range: 6-23

Risk Categories:

  • 19-23: No identified risk - routine skin care
  • 15-18: At risk - implement preventive measures
  • 13-14: Moderate risk - more aggressive prevention
  • 10-12: High risk - intensive prevention
  • ≤9: Very high risk - aggressive, multifaceted prevention

Prevention Strategies: Frequent repositioning, pressure relief devices, skin care, nutrition support, moisture management, mobility enhancement

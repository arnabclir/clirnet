Naegele's Rule & Dating Methods
Naegele's Rule Calculation: 1. Start with first day of LMP 2. Add 1 year 3. Subtract 3 months 4. Add 7 days OR simply add 280 days to LMP date Normal pregnancy length: 37-42 weeks - Term pregnancy: 37-42 weeks - Preterm: <37 weeks - Post-term: >42 weeks Accuracy of Dating: - Clinical examination: ±3-4 weeks - First trimester ultrasound: ±3-5 days - Second trimester ultrasound: ±1-2 weeks - Third trimester ultrasound: ±3-4 weeks Important Notes: - EDD is an estimate, not a specific delivery date - Only ~5% deliver on exact EDD - 90% deliver within 2 weeks of EDD - Ultrasound in first trimester most accurate - Consider menstrual cycle regularity - Use clinical judgment with patient history

Modified Geneva PE Score
Modified Geneva Score Components: Clinical factors: - Age > 65: 1 point - Cancer history: 2 points - Heart failure/chronic respiratory disease: 3 points - Clinical signs of DVT: 3 points - Hemoptysis: 2 points - Clinical PE signs: 5 points - Heart rate ≥ 100: 3 points Score Interpretation: - 0-3: Low probability (8% PE rate) - 4-10: Moderate probability (26%) - > 10: High probability (74%) Used to guide need for CT pulmonary angiography (CTPA)

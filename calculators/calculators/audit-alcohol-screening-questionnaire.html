AUDIT Alcohol Screening Questionnaire

Patient Education - Assess alcohol use and related risks

Instructions: Answer the following 10 questions about your alcohol use. Each question has multiple choice answers with point values. Your total score will help determine your risk level.

AUDIT Questionnaire

10-item screening tool for hazardous alcohol use

1. How often do you have a drink containing alcohol?
2. How many drinks do you have on a typical day when you are drinking?
3. How often do you have 6 or more drinks on one occasion?
4. How often during the last year have you found that you were unable to stop drinking once you had started?
5. How often during the last year have you neglected your responsibilities because of drinking?
6. Have you or someone else been hurt because of your drinking?
7. Has a relative, doctor, or other health worker been concerned about your drinking or suggested you cut down?
8. Have you ever tried to cut down on drinking and found you could not?
9. Have you ever had a drink first thing in the morning to steady your nerves or get rid of a hangover?
10. Have you ever had memory blackouts or been unable to remember what happened the night before because of drinking?

Scoring Guide

Score Range Risk Level Interpretation
0-7 Low Risk Alcohol use is within safe limits
8-15 Moderate Risk Hazardous alcohol use; consider reduction
16-19 High Risk Harmful or dependent alcohol use; seek professional help
20+ Very High Risk Likely alcohol dependence; urgent professional intervention needed

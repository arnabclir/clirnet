APRI Score (SI Units)
AST to Platelet Ratio Index for Liver Fibrosis
About This Calculator: The APRI score uses AST and platelet count to estimate liver fibrosis, particularly in chronic hepatitis C and HIV patients.
Formula (SI Units):
APRI = (AST [U/L] / Upper Limit Normal) / Platelet Count [10⁹/L] × 100
Upper Limit Normal for AST = 40 U/L
Serum AST level in U/L (SI units)
Platelet count in 10⁹/L (SI units). Normal: 150-400

BMI Categories

BMI Range Category Health Risk
Below 18.5 Underweight Risk of nutritional deficiency and other health issues
18.5 - 24.9 Normal Weight Healthy weight range
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight Increased risk of health complications
30.0 - 34.9 Obese (Class I) Significant health risks
35.0 - 39.9 Obese (Class II) Very high health risks
40.0+ Obese (Class III) Extreme health risks; urgent intervention needed

Formula

BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ Height (m)²

Example: If you weigh 70 kg and are 1.75 m (175 cm) tall:
BMI = 70 ÷ (1.75)² = 70 ÷ 3.0625 = 22.9 (Normal Weight)

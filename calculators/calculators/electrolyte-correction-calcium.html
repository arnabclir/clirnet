Calcium Correction for Hypoalbuminemia

Adjust for Albumin Binding Effect

g/dL
Clinical Use: About 40% of serum calcium is bound to albumin. Hypoalbuminemia artificially lowers total calcium, but ionized (free) calcium may be normal. This calculator corrects total calcium to a standard albumin level of 4.0 g/dL.
Formula (Payne):
Corrected Ca2+ = Total Ca + 0.8 × (4.0 - Serum Albumin)

Formula (Alternative):
Corrected Ca2+ = Total Ca + 0.8 × (4.0 - Albumin) or 1.0 × (4.0 - Albumin)

About Du Bois Formula

CHEMOTHERAPYThe Du Bois formula is a classic and widely validated method for BSA calculation. Formula: BSA (m²) = (Height (cm)^0.725 × Weight (kg)^0.425) × 0.007184. Preferred for chemotherapy dosing accuracy.

IMPORTANT: This calculator is for informational purposes. Always verify dosing with institutional protocols, pharmacy, and oncology specialists before administration.

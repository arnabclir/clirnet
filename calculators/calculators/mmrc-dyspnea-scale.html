mMRC Scale Information

mMRC = Grade 0-4 based on dyspnea triggers

  • Grade 0-1: Mild dyspnea, minimal functional limitation
  • Grade 2: Moderate dyspnea, some functional limitation
  • Grade 3-4: Severe dyspnea, significant functional limitation
  • Component of BODE index for COPD prognosis
  • Guides intensity of pulmonary rehabilitation

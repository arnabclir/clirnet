Model Details:
- BCSC (Breast Cancer Surveillance Consortium) consortium data from 1,304,147 screening mammograms
- Risk factors: age, BMI, race/ethnicity, breast density, menopausal status, family history, HRT use
- Breast density is a significant independent risk factor
- Model validation: AUC 0.69 (improved discrimination over Gail model)
- Applied to asymptomatic women undergoing screening mammography

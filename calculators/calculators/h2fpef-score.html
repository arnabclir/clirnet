H2FPEF Score

Heart Failure with Preserved Ejection Fraction Probability

Clinical Context:

H2FPEF predicts probability of HFpEF in patients with dyspnea. Helps differentiate HFpEF from other causes of dyspnea.

H2FPEF Components
Scoring:
  • H: Hypertension history + ≥2 antihypertensive meds = up to 2 points
  • F: Atrial fibrillation on ECG = 1 point
  • P: Pulmonary edema on CXR = 1 point
  • E: E/e' ratio on echo = 0-2 points
  • F: LVEF >50% = points based on percentage

Clinical Note: H2FPEF helps identify patients with dyspnea who likely have HFpEF. Confirmed diagnosis requires echocardiography showing preserved LVEF with evidence of diastolic dysfunction.

CAT Scoring Guide

CAT = Sum of 8 items (0-5 each)

Total Score Range: 0-40 points

  • 0-10: Minimal impact
  • 11-20: Mild impact
  • 21-30: Moderate impact
  • 31-40: Very high impact
  • Used to guide treatment intensity and monitor response to therapy

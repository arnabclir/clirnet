Malignant Hyperthermia Indicators

Clinical Screening for Susceptibility
Screens for potential malignant hyperthermia (MH) susceptibility. Positive history requires avoidance of triggering agents.
Malignant Hyperthermia Risk Assessment
-
MH Risk Factors:
- Family history (most important)
- Unexplained fever during anesthesia
- Tachycardia/arrhythmias
- Elevated CK, myoglobinuria
- Muscular build

Triggering Agents to AVOID:
- ALL volatile anesthetics
- Succinylcholine

Safe Anesthesia:
- IV induction (propofol)
- Non-depolarizing paralytics
- Non-volatile agents
- Dantrolene sodium available

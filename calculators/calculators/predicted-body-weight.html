PBW Formula

Males: PBW (kg) = 50 + 0.91 × (height - 152.4)

Females: PBW (kg) = 45.5 + 0.91 × (height - 152.4)

  • Used to calculate lung-protective tidal volumes
  • Standard is 6-8 mL/kg PBW for mechanical ventilation
  • Reduces ventilator-induced lung injury (VILI)
  • Lower tidal volumes improve ARDS outcomes

Scoring Reference
TIMI Risk Score Components (7 points total):
  • Age ≥65 = 1 point
  • ≥3 CAD risk factors = 1 point
  • Prior coronary stenosis = 1 point
  • Aspirin use in past 7 days = 1 point
  • Severe angina (≥2 episodes in 24h) = 1 point
  • ST changes ≥0.5mm = 1 point
  • Elevated cardiac markers = 1 point

Clinical Note: Higher scores indicate need for more aggressive antiplatelet therapy, anticoagulation, and consideration of early invasive strategy.

Sports Medicine Immunology Notes
  • Exercise-Induced Leukocytosis: WBC elevation is normal during/after exercise
  • Open Window Hypothesis: Brief period of immune suppression post-exercise
  • Overtraining Markers: Chronic elevation of WBC/neutrophils may indicate overtraining
  • IgG Levels: Important for monitoring infection risk in endurance athletes
  • Training Adaptations: Serial monitoring helps assess immune adaptation to training

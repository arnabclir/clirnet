Formulas:

Ideal Body Weight (IBW):
Males: IBW = 50 + 2.3 × (Height in inches - 60)
Females: IBW = 45.5 + 2.3 × (Height in inches - 60)

Adjusted Body Weight (AdjBW):
AdjBW = IBW + 0.4 × (Actual Weight - IBW)

Clinical Applications:
- Propofol dosing (use IBW)
- Rocuronium dosing (use IBW or AdjBW for obese)
- Cefazolin dosing (use AdjBW if >30% overweight)
- Ideal for BMI 20-30; consider AdjBW for BMI >30

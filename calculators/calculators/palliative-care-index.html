About These Calculators

These evidence-based clinical tools are designed specifically for palliative care practitioners to assess patient symptoms, disease progression, and quality of life. Each calculator provides rapid risk stratification and clinical interpretation to guide treatment decisions and support shared decision-making.

Key Features:

  • Evidence-based scoring systems widely used in palliative and critical care medicine
  • Real-time calculations with clinical interpretation and recommendations
  • Responsive design for use on desktop and mobile devices
  • Emphasis on symptom assessment and quality of life measures
  • Support for prognosis estimation and care planning

Clinical Disclaimer

These calculators are provided for educational and clinical decision support purposes only. They should not replace clinical judgment or be used as the sole basis for treatment decisions. Always consider the complete clinical context, patient preferences, and multidisciplinary team input. Results should be interpreted by qualified healthcare professionals.

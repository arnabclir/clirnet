Maintenance Fluid Calculation

Daily Requirements (Holliday-Segar Method)
Instructions: Enter the child's weight in kilograms. The calculator will compute daily maintenance fluid requirements using the Holliday-Segar method.
Holliday-Segar Formula (Daily):
  • First 10 kg: 100 mL/kg/day
  • Next 10 kg: 50 mL/kg/day
  • Each kg above 20 kg: 20 mL/kg/day
Range: 1-100 kg

Score Interpretation
AUDIT Score Interpretation: 0-7 points: Low Risk - Abstinent or minimal alcohol consumption - Continue current drinking pattern - No intervention needed at this time 8-15 points: Hazardous Alcohol Use - Increased risk of harmful consequences - Simple advice recommended - Consider brief intervention strategies - Monitor at future visits 16-19 points: Harmful Alcohol Use - Significant risk of alcohol-related harms - Brief counseling and behavioral change needed - Consider referral to specialist if available 20+ points: Alcohol Dependence Likely - High probability of alcohol dependence - Specialist referral strongly recommended - Medical management may be necessary - Consider detoxification support

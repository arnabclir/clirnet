Ranson Criteria
Ranson Criteria for Acute Pancreatitis: At Admission (present if): - Age > 55 years - WBC > 16,000 - Glucose > 200 mg/dL - AST > 250 IU/L - LDH > 350 IU/L At 48 Hours (present if): - BUN rise > 5 mg/dL - Calcium < 8 mg/dL - PaO2 < 60 mmHg - Base deficit > 4 mEq/L - Fluid sequestration > 6L - Albumin < 3.2 g/dL Mortality Risk: - 0-2 criteria: 0-3% mortality - 3-4 criteria: 15-20% - 5-6 criteria: 40-50% - 7-11 criteria: 80-100%

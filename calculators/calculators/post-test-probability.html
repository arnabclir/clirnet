Likelihood Ratios & Calculations
Post-Test Probability Calculation: 1. Convert pre-test probability to pre-test odds: Pre-test odds = P / (1 - P) 2. Calculate post-test odds: Post-test odds = Pre-test odds Ã— Likelihood Ratio 3. Convert post-test odds back to probability: Post-test probability = Odds / (1 + Odds) Likelihood Ratio Interpretation: LR+ (Positive Test): - >10: Significant increase in disease probability - 5-10: Moderate increase - 2-5: Small increase - 1-2: Minimal increase - =1: No change in probability LR- (Negative Test): - <0.1: Significant decrease in disease probability - 0.1-0.2: Moderate decrease - 0.2-0.5: Small decrease - 0.5-1: Minimal decrease - =1: No change in probability Clinical Application: - Use to update clinical suspicion after test results - Helps decide: Treat, test further, or reassure - Combines clinical judgment with test accuracy - Evidence-based approach to diagnosis

Spot Urine Protein-to-Creatinine Ratio

Children (6 months - 18 years)

Clinical Use: Pediatric reference ranges differ from adults. Normal pediatric proteinuria is <4 mg/kg/day. Spot urine PCR is the preferred screening method in children.
Formula:
PCR = Urine Protein / Urine Creatinine
Pediatric normal: <0.2 g/g Cr

Pediatric Proteinuria Classification
Normal: <0.2 g/g Cr (<4 mg/kg/day)
Asymptomatic proteinuria: 0.2-2 g/g Cr
Nephrotic: >3.5 g/g Cr or >40 mg/kg/day

10-Year Risk (ACC/AHA 2013 Pooled Cohort)

Clinical Context:

ACC/AHA 2013 calculator estimates 10-year risk of atherosclerotic cardiovascular disease (ASCVD) in adults without prior ASCVD. Guides intensity of prevention strategies.

Risk Factors
Key Risk Factors Assessed:
  • Age and sex
  • Race/ethnicity
  • Total cholesterol and HDL
  • Systolic blood pressure
  • Blood pressure medication use
  • Diabetes status
  • Smoking status

Note: Based on Pooled Cohort Equations. Valid for non-Hispanic whites and African Americans age 40-79 without known ASCVD.

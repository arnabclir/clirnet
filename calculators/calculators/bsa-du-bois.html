BSA Calculator (Du Bois)

Body Surface Area - Du Bois Method
The Du Bois method is an alternative BSA formula, historically important and still used in some clinical settings.
BSA Calculation Results
-
Du Bois Formula:
BSA (m²) = 0.007184 × Height(cm)^0.725 × Weight(kg)^0.425

Formula Comparison:
- Du Bois: More precise for extremes of body composition
- Mosteller: Simpler, more commonly used

Clinical Applications:
- Chemotherapy dosing
- Burn fluid resuscitation
- Hemodynamic calculations
- Drug dosing adjustments

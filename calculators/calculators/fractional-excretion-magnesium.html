Fractional Excretion of Magnesium (FEMg)

SI and Conventional Units

Use: FEMg helps evaluate the kidney's ability to conserve magnesium and can indicate hypomagnesemia of renal origin.
Formula:
FEMg (%) = [(Urine Mg × Serum Cr) / (Serum Mg × Urine Cr)] × 100

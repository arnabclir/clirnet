Creatinine Clearance (Measured)

Adults and Children 1 Month and Older

m²
Standard adult BSA: 1.73 m²
Collection Instructions: Obtain a timed 24-hour urine collection for accurate creatinine clearance measurement. Standard BSA is 1.73 m².
Formula:
Creatinine Clearance (mL/min/1.73m²) = (Urine Cr × Urine Volume) / (Plasma Cr × Collection Time × BSA)

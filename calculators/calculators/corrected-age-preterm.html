Corrected Age Calculator

For Preterm Infants (Up to 24 months)
Instructions: Enter the infant's gestational age at birth and current chronological age. The corrected (or adjusted) age accounts for prematurity and is used for developmental assessments until age 2-3 years.
Corrected Age Formula:
Corrected Age = Chronological Age - (40 weeks - Gestational Age)
Where 40 weeks is full term
Range: 22-42 weeks

Clinical Use:
  • Use corrected age for developmental milestones and growth charts until 24-36 months
  • Plot growth parameters (weight, length, head circumference) on corrected age
  • Assess developmental milestones using corrected age
  • Use for nutritional assessment and feeding guidelines
  • Stop adjustment after age 3-4 years or when catch-up growth is complete

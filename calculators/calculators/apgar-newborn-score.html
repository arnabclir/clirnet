Clinical Interpretation
  • 8-10: Normal - No intervention needed
  • 5-7: Moderately abnormal - May need intervention
  • 0-4: Severely abnormal - Immediate intervention required
  • Note: Score should be reassessed at 5 and 10 minutes
Reference

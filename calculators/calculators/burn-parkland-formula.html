Burn Fluid Resuscitation

Parkland Formula
Parkland formula calculates fluid needs for severe burn injuries. Apply only first 24 hours; adjust based on urine output.
Parkland Formula Results
Total 24-Hour Fluid
-
First 8 Hours (Ringer's Lactate)
-
Remaining 16 Hours
-
Hourly Rate (First 8h)
-
Formula:
Total 24h = 4 mL × TBSA (%) × Weight (kg)
Use Ringer's Lactate
First 8h = 50% of total
Last 16h = 50% of total

Guidelines:
- Target urine output: 0.5 mL/kg/h
- Adjust based on response
- Add D5NS for maintenance after 24h
- Monitor for fluid overload (ARP)

SCORING ALGORITHMS:
===================

STOP-Bang: 
- Each item = 0 or 1 point
- Total: 0-8 points
- Interpretation: <3 (Low), 3-4 (Moderate), ≥5 (High)

Epworth:
- Each item = 0-3 points
- Total: 0-24 points
- Interpretation: 0-5 (Normal), 6-10 (Mild), 11-15 (Moderate), 16-24 (Severe)

Sleep Problems:
- Multiple scoring domains
- Total: 0-30+ points
- Interpretation: ≤5 (Minimal), 6-12 (Mild), 13-20 (Moderate), >20 (Significant)

Clinical Diagnosis of Endocarditis
Duke Criteria for Infective Endocarditis
About This Calculator: The Duke Criteria provide a standardized approach to diagnose infective endocarditis based on clinical, laboratory, and echocardiographic findings.
Major Criteria (2 points each)
Viridans streptococci, S. aureus, HACEK, Enterococci
Vegetation or prosthetic valve abnormality on echocardiogram
Minor Criteria (1 point each)
Septic emboli, Janeway lesions, splinter hemorrhages
Osler nodes, Roth spots, glomerulonephritis
1/4 or 3+ of 4 blood cultures with typical organisms
Echo findings consistent with endocarditis but not meeting major criteria

APRI Score (Conventional Units)
AST to Platelet Ratio Index for Liver Fibrosis
About This Calculator: The APRI score using conventional units (platelets in thousands/μL) for assessing liver fibrosis.
Formula (Conventional Units):
APRI = (AST [U/L] / Upper Limit Normal) / Platelet Count [thousands/μL] × 100
Upper Limit Normal for AST = 40 U/L
Serum AST level in U/L
Platelet count in thousands/μL. Normal: 150-400

Fibrosis Stage Interpretation
Score Cutoff Values
APRI < 0.5: No/Minimal fibrosis (F0-F1)
APRI 0.5-1.5: Mild to moderate fibrosis (F1-F2)
APRI 1.5-2.3: Moderate to advanced fibrosis (F2-F3)
APRI > 2.3: Advanced fibrosis/Cirrhosis (F3-F4)

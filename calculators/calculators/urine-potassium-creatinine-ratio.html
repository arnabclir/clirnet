Urine Potassium-to-Creatinine Ratio

UKCR Assessment

Clinical Use: Spot urine UKCR estimates daily potassium excretion. Used in assessment of hypokalemia and hyperkalemia etiology.
Formula:
UKCR = Urine K+ / Urine Creatinine

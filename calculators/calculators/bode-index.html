BODE Index Components

BODE = B (0-1) + O (0-3) + D (0-3) + E (0-3)

Total Score Range: 0-10 points

  • B: BMI - measures nutritional status
  • O: Obstruction - FEV1 severity of airflow limitation
  • D: Dyspnea - perceived limitation from breathlessness
  • E: Exercise capacity - 6MWT functional limitation

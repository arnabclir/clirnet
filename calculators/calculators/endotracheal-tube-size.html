Endotracheal Tube Size (Children 1-8 years)

Calculate appropriate ETT size for pediatric patients
ETT sizing is critical for safe pediatric airway management. Use age as primary guide; adjust for body habitus.

Cole Formula:
ETT size (mm) = (Age in years / 4) + 4

Insertion Depth at Teeth:
Depth (cm) = (Age in years / 2) + 12

Uncuffed Tubes:
 - Recommended for age < 8-10 years
- Reduces post-extubation croup
- Lower air leak risk with proper sizing

Cuffed Tubes:
- Consider for age > 8 years
- Allows ARDS/PEEP management
- Use size 0.5 mm smaller than uncuffed

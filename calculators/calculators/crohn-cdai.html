CDAI Calculation
CDAI = Sum of weighted factors Weighted by 2: - Liquid/soft stools per day Weighted by 5: - Abdominal pain (0-3) - General well-being (0-4) Weighted by 20: - Complications (0-5) - Hemoglobin adjustment - Hematocrit adjustment Score Interpretation: - < 150: Remission - 150-220: Mild activity - 220-450: Moderate activity - > 450: Severe activity

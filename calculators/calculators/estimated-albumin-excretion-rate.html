Estimated Albumin Excretion Rate

From Spot Urine Albumin-to-Creatinine Ratio

Method: This estimates 24-hour albumin excretion based on spot urine ACR and sex-specific daily creatinine excretion. The calculation assumes normal daily creatinine excretion varies by sex and body weight.
Formula:
Daily Cr excretion = 28 × weight(kg) - 16 (males), 22 × weight(kg) - 16 (females)
ACR = Urine Albumin / Urine Creatinine
Estimated Daily Albumin = ACR × Daily Cr excretion

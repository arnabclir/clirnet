Understanding Your BMI

Your Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on your height and weight. It's a simple screening tool that applies to adult men and women.

Health Tips
  • BMI is a screening tool, not a diagnostic measure of body fatness
  • Maintain a healthy weight through balanced diet and regular exercise
  • Consult your healthcare provider about your BMI category
  • Individual factors like muscle mass, bone density, and age matter
Formula Used:

BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ [Height (m)]²

A-a Gradient Formula

PAO₂ = (Patm - PH₂O) × FiO₂ - (PaCO₂/0.8)

Patm = 760 mmHg, PH₂O = 47 mmHg

A-a gradient = PAO₂ - PaO₂

  • Normal A-a gradient: <10 mmHg on room air
  • Increases ~2.5 mmHg per decade of age
  • Indicates presence of intrapulmonary shunting if elevated
  • Differentiates pulmonary vs non-pulmonary causes of hypoxemia

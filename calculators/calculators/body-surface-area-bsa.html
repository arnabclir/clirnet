Body Surface Area (BSA) Calculator

Mosteller Method
Instructions: Enter the child's height in centimeters and weight in kilograms. The Mosteller method is the most commonly used formula for pediatric BSA calculation.
Mosteller Formula:
BSA (m²) = √[(Height (cm) × Weight (kg)) / 3600]
Range: 30-220 cm
Range: 1-200 kg

Clinical Applications:
  • Chemotherapy dosing: Most anticancer drugs are dosed based on BSA
  • Fluid resuscitation: Used in burn calculations (Parkland formula)
  • Drug dosing: Used to calculate pediatric doses of many medications
  • Cardiac output: Used to calculate cardiac index
  • Renal function: Used in some renal function calculators
Approximate BSA by Age:
Newborn (3.5 kg) 0.2 m²
6 months (8 kg) 0.4 m²
1 year (10 kg) 0.5 m²
2 years (15 kg) 0.6 m²
5 years (20 kg) 0.8 m²
10 years (30 kg) 1.1 m²
Adult (70 kg) 1.8 m²
Important Notes:
  • Always verify BSA values against institutional protocols before using for drug dosing
  • The Mosteller method is most accurate in children between 30 and 200 cm in height
  • For children with extreme measurements, consult pediatric specialists
  • Different formulas (DuBois, Boyd, Haycock) may be used in specific situations

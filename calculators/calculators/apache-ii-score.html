APACHE II Formula

APACHE II = Acute Physiology Score (0-60) + Age Score + Chronic Health Score

Score Range: 0-71 points

  • Each physiologic variable scored based on how far from normal
  • Higher scores indicate greater illness severity
  • Mortality risk increases exponentially with score

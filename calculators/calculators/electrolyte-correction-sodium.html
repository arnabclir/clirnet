Sodium Correction for Hyperglycemia

Adjust for Osmotic Effect of Glucose

mEq/L
Clinical Use: Corrects for the osmotic effect of hyperglycemia on sodium concentration. Hyperglycemia can cause apparent hyponatremia (pseudohyponatremia).
Formula:
Corrected Na+ = Measured Na+ + 0.016 × (Glucose - 100)
Alternative: Corrected Na+ = Measured Na+ + 0.024 × (Glucose - 100)

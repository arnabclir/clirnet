Kt/V Interpretation: Measures dialysis adequacy. K = dialyzer clearance, t = time, V = volume of distribution of urea. Goal: Kt/V ≥1.4 for adequate hemodialysis.
Daugirdas Formula (2nd generation):
Kt/V = -ln(R - 0.008*t) + (4 - 3.5*R) * UF/W
R = (C_post / C_pre)
UF = ultrafiltration volume, W = post-dialysis weight

Blood Ethanol Concentration

Estimate BAC from consumption
Estimates BAC based on drinks consumed. Account for metabolism rate (~15 mg/dL/hour).
Blood Ethanol Concentration
-
Formula:
Widmark: BAC = (Drinks × 5.14 / (Weight(kg) × r)) - (0.015 × Hours)
r = 0.55 (female), 0.68 (male)

Impairment Levels:
- 0.02-0.03: Mild impairment
- 0.05-0.08: Moderate impairment
- 0.08+: Legal intoxication
- 0.35+: Severe intoxication

Cockcroft-Gault Creatinine Clearance

Conventional and SI Units

Cockcroft-Gault Formula: The most commonly used equation for estimating creatinine clearance. Less accurate for obese patients, older adults, and those with kidney disease.
Conventional Units:
CrCl (mL/min) = [(140 - age) × weight (kg)] / (72 × Cr) × 0.85 if female

SI Units:
CrCl (mL/min) = [(140 - age) × weight (kg)] / Cr (μmol/L) × 0.85 if female

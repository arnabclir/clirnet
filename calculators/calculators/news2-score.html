NEWS2 Score Components
NEWS2 Score Components (scoring based on vital sign abnormality): Respiration Rate: 0-3 points (by range) Oxygen Saturation: 0-3 points (by level) Temperature: 0-3 points (< 35, 35-36.1, 36.2-38, 38.1-39, ≥ 39.1) Heart Rate: 0-3 points (by range) Systolic BP: 0-3 points (by range) Consciousness: 0 or 3 points (Alert vs abnormal) Supplemental Oxygen: 0 or 2 points Score Interpretation: - 0-4: Low risk (routine care) - 5-6: Medium risk (urgent review) - ≥ 7: High risk (emergency review) Used for rapid assessment in emergency/hospital settings

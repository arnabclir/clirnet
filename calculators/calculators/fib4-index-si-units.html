FIB-4 Index (SI Units)
Fibrosis-4 Index for Liver Fibrosis Assessment
About This Calculator: The FIB-4 index uses age, AST, ALT, and platelet count to assess liver fibrosis, particularly useful in HCV and HIV co-infection.
Formula (SI Units):
FIB-4 = (Age × AST [U/L]) / (Platelet Count [10⁹/L] × √ALT [U/L])

FIB-4 < 1.30: Low risk of advanced fibrosis (AUROC ~0.80)

FIB-4 > 2.67: High risk of advanced fibrosis (cirrhosis)

Oxygenation Index Formula

OI = (FiO₂ × MAP) / PaO₂

  • OI <5: Mild hypoxemia, good prognosis
  • OI 5-10: Moderate hypoxemia, intermediate prognosis
  • OI 10-15: Severe hypoxemia, poor prognosis
  • OI >15: Very severe hypoxemia, likely fatal without support
  • Incorporates ventilator settings and blood gas; more comprehensive than P/F ratio

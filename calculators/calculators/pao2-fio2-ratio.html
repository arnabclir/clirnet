P/F Ratio Assessment

P/F Ratio = PaO₂ (mmHg) / FiO₂ (as fraction)

Example: PaO₂ 60 mmHg on FiO₂ 40% = 60/0.4 = 150

  • P/F >300: Normal oxygenation
  • P/F 200-300: Mild hypoxemia
  • P/F 100-200: Moderate to severe ARDS
  • P/F <100: Severe ARDS with high mortality
  • Core criterion for ARDS diagnosis (Berlin definition)
  • Must have bilateral opacities on imaging and appropriate clinical context

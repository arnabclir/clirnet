BSA Calculator (Mosteller)

Body Surface Area - Mosteller Method
BSA is used for drug dosing calculations and metabolic assessments. The Mosteller formula is most commonly used.
BSA Calculation Results
-
Mosteller Formula:
BSA (m²) = √[Height(cm) × Weight(kg) / 3600]

BSA Reference Values:
- Adults: 1.5-2.0 m² (average 1.7-1.9 m²)
- Children vary by age

Clinical Applications:
- Chemotherapy dosing
- Burn fluid resuscitation
- Cardiac output normalization

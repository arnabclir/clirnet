GFR (Race-Free CKD-EPI eGFRcr)

Based on Serum Creatinine Only

2021 CKD-EPI Equation (Race-Free): This updated equation removes the race adjustment factor that was found to propagate healthcare disparities. Valid for age 18+.
Formula (Race-Free CKD-EPI eGFRcr):
eGFR = 142 × min(Scr/κ, 1)^α × max(Scr/κ, 1)^-1.2 × 0.9938^age × [1.1 if female]

Where κ = 0.7 (female), 0.9 (male) and α = -0.241 (female), -0.302 (male)

CKD Stages
Stage 1: eGFR ≥90 (normal/high)
Stage 2: eGFR 60-89 (mildly decreased)
Stage 3a: eGFR 45-59 (mildly to moderately decreased)
Stage 3b: eGFR 30-44 (moderately to severely decreased)
Stage 4: eGFR 15-29 (severely decreased)
Stage 5: eGFR <15 (kidney failure)

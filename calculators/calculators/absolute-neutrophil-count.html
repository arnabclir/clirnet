ANC Calculation Formula

ANC = WBC Count (x10³) × (% Neutrophils + % Bands) ÷ 100

Infection Risk Categories:

  • ANC ≥ 2000 (≥2.0): Normal - Low infection risk
  • ANC 1500-1999 (1.5-1.99): Mild neutropenia - Minimal risk
  • ANC 1000-1499 (1.0-1.49): Moderate neutropenia - Moderate risk
  • ANC <1000 (<1.0): Severe neutropenia - High infection risk

Clinical Notes:

  • Band neutrophils represent immature neutrophils (left shift)
  • Severe neutropenia (ANC <500) considered critical
  • Requires prophylaxis and isolation precautions

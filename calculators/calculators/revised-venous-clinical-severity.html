REVCS Score Classification

Score Range: 0-36 points

Severity Classification:

  • 0-2: No disease - Normal venous examination
  • 3-7: Mild disease - Early signs of venous disease
  • 8-19: Moderate disease - Significant venous insufficiency
  • ≥20: Severe disease - Advanced CVD with complications

Note: Score ≥1 point with active ulcer = severe disease

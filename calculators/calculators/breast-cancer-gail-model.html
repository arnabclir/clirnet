Model Details:
- Based on National Surgical Adjuvant Breast and Bowel Project (NSABP) data
- Risk factors include age, age at menarche, age at first birth, family history, and prior biopsies
- Model uses logistic regression with race-specific adjustments (2018 update)
- Validation: AUC 0.62 (reasonable discrimination for screening purposes)
- Limitations: Does not account for BRCA mutations, very strong family histories, or hormone use patterns

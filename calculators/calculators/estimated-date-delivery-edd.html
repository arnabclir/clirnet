Clinical Considerations
  • LMP method: Assumes 28-day cycle with ovulation on day 14. Error margin ±3-5 days
  • Naegele's Rule: LMP + 280 days (40 weeks)
  • Ultrasound accuracy: Best in first trimester (±3-5 days), decreases as pregnancy advances
  • Delivery typically occurs between 37-42 weeks
  • Use best available dating from earliest assessment

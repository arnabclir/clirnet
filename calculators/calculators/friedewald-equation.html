Friedewald Equation

LDL Cholesterol Estimation from Lipid Panel

Clinical Context:

Friedewald equation estimates LDL cholesterol from total cholesterol, HDL, and triglycerides. Direct LDL measurement is preferred for accurate assessment.

Normal: >40 mg/dL (M), >50 mg/dL (F)
Normal: <150 mg/dL

Friedewald Formula
LDL Calculation:

LDL = Total Chol - HDL - (Triglycerides / 5)

VLDL Approximation:

VLDL = Triglycerides / 5
(Valid when TG < 400 mg/dL)

Clinical Notes:

  • Valid only when triglycerides < 400 mg/dL
  • Less accurate with very low or very high TG
  • Direct LDL measurement preferred for precise assessment
  • Use for screening and CVD risk assessment

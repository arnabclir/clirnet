About CAC Scoring
Coronary Artery Calcium Categories:
  • 0 (None): No detectable CAC - Very low risk
  • 1-100 (Minimal): Minimal CAC - Low risk
  • 101-300 (Mild): Mild CAC - Intermediate risk
  • 301-400 (Moderate): Moderate CAC - Higher risk
  • ≥400 (Extensive): Extensive CAC - Highest risk

Note: MESA incorporates CAC for more precise risk stratification, especially useful in borderline-risk patients. CAC score is obtained via coronary CT imaging.

Calculation Methods & Health Impact
Smoking Cost Calculation: Daily Cost = (Cigarettes per day / Cigarettes per pack) × Cost per pack Annual Cost = Daily Cost × 365 Total Cost = Annual Cost × Years smoking Health Impact Estimates: - Reduces life expectancy by ~10 years - Increases cardiovascular disease risk by 2-4x - Increases cancer risk by 10-25x (depending on cancer type) - Increases COPD risk by 10-15x - Reduces average lifespan: ~15 minutes per cigarette - Secondhand smoke exposure affects family members Smoking Cessation Benefits: - Year 1: Risk of coronary disease drops significantly - Year 2: Stroke risk approaches non-smoker level - Year 10: Lung cancer mortality risk drops to 50% of smoker - 10+ years: Can gain back most lost life years Financial Motivation: Use these estimates to help patients understand the financial burden of smoking and benefits of cessation. Many quit successfully with proper support.

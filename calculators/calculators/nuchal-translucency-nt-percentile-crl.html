Important Clinical Information
  • NT screening window: 11 weeks 3 days to 14 weeks 3 days
  • CRL range: 38-84 mm corresponds to appropriate gestational age
  • Normal NT: Usually less than 3 mm, varies with CRL (proportional)
  • Increased NT: > 95th percentile or > 3.5 mm associated with higher aneuploidy risk
  • Combined screening: NT + maternal age + biomarkers (PAPP-A, hCG) improves detection
  • Quality of ultrasound and operator experience critical for accurate measurement
  • Additional testing (cfDNA, amniocentesis) recommended if abnormal

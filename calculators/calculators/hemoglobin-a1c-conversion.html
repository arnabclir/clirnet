Conversion Formulas
A1C (%) to mmol/mol:
A1C (mmol/mol) = 10.93 × A1C (%) - 23.5
A1C (%) to Average Glucose:
Avg Glucose (mg/dL) = [A1C (%) - 2.15] / 0.015
A1C (%) to Average Glucose (mmol/L):
Avg Glucose (mmol/L) = 0.0556 × Avg Glucose (mg/dL) - 0.1167

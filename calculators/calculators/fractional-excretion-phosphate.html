Fractional Excretion of Phosphate (FEPi)

Renal Phosphate Handling Assessment

Use: FEPi helps evaluate renal tubular handling of phosphate and is useful in differentiating causes of hypophosphatemia.
Formula:
FEPi (%) = [(Urine Pi × Serum Cr) / (Serum Pi × Urine Cr)] × 100

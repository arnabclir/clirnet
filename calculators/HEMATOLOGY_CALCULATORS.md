# Hematology Specialty Calculators - Complete Collection

## Overview
15 pure HTML5/CSS3/Bootstrap 5 calculators for hematology specialty assessment and risk stratification.

## Calculators Created

### 1. 4 Ts Score (4-ts-score.html)
**Purpose:** Heparin-Induced Thrombocytopenia (HIT) pretest probability assessment
**Features:**
- Evaluates thrombocytopenia severity, timing, clinical events, and temporal relationship
- Risk stratification: Low, Intermediate, High
- Clinical guidance for HIT testing and management

### 2. CNS-IPI Score (cns-ipi-score.html)
**Purpose:** Central Nervous System relapse risk in DLBCL (R-CHOP treated)
**Features:**
- Evaluates age, LDH, ECOG, stage, and kidney/adrenal involvement
- 3-year CNS relapse risk quantification
- Prophylaxis recommendations based on risk category

### 3. CIBMTR Chronic GVHD Score (cibmtr-chronic-gvhd-score.html)
**Purpose:** Chronic Graft-versus-Host Disease risk assessment
**Features:**
- Considers donor type, age, gender effects, prior acute GVHD, CMV reactivation
- Risk stratification for post-transplant management
- Prophylaxis guidance

### 4. HAS-BLED Score (has-bled-score.html)
**Purpose:** Bleeding risk in anticoagulated patients
**Features:**
- 8-component assessment (Hypertension, Renal/Liver, Stroke, Bleeding, Age, Medications, Alcohol)
- 1-year major bleeding risk quantification
- Anticoagulation management recommendations

### 5. DVT Wells Score (dvt-wells-score.html)
**Purpose:** Deep Vein Thrombosis probability assessment
**Features:**
- Clinical assessment with negative points for alternative diagnosis
- Risk stratification: Low, Intermediate, High
- Imaging recommendations based on probability

### 6. GIPSS Score (gipss-score.html)
**Purpose:** Genetic Prognostic Index for Myelofibrosis stratification
**Features:**
- IPSS component, TP53 and ASXL1 mutation status
- Favorable to high-risk stratification
- OS estimates and treatment guidance

### 7. International Prognostic Index (ipi-non-hodgkin-lymphoma.html)
**Purpose:** Non-Hodgkin Lymphoma prognostic assessment
**Features:**
- 5 components: Age, LDH, ECOG, Stage, Extranodal sites
- 5-year overall survival estimation
- Risk-adapted treatment recommendations

### 8. International Prognostic Score (ips-hodgkin-lymphoma.html)
**Purpose:** Hodgkin Lymphoma prognostic stratification
**Features:**
- 6-component assessment with clinical and laboratory parameters
- 5-year freedom from progression (FFP) estimation
- Treatment escalation guidance based on risk

### 9. Khorana Score (khorana-score.html)
**Purpose:** Venous Thromboembolism (VTE) risk in cancer patients
**Features:**
- 5 components: Cancer type, platelet count, hemoglobin, WBC, BMI
- VTE incidence risk quantification
- Thromboprophylaxis recommendations

### 10. ANC Calculator (anc-calculator.html)
**Purpose:** Absolute Neutrophil Count calculation
**Features:**
- WBC × (% Segments + % Bands) formula
- Classification: Normal to profound neutropenia
- Infection risk guidance and management

### 11. Reticulocyte Production Index (rpi-calculator.html)
**Purpose:** Bone marrow response assessment to anemia
**Features:**
- Corrects reticulocyte count for hematocrit
- Maturation time adjustment based on anemia severity
- Evaluation of bone marrow adequacy

### 12. Transferrin Saturation (tsat-calculator.html)
**Purpose:** Iron metabolism assessment
**Features:**
- TSAT = (Serum Iron ÷ TIBC) × 100
- Classification: Deficiency to severe overload
- Iron metabolism disorder guidance

### 13. MIPI Score (mipi-score.html)
**Purpose:** Mantle Cell Lymphoma International Prognostic Index
**Features:**
- Age, ECOG, LDH, and WBC-based scoring
- Risk stratification with survival estimates
- Treatment intensity recommendations

### 14. MDACC MDS Score (mdacc-mds-score.html)
**Purpose:** MD Anderson Cancer Center MDS prognostic model
**Features:**
- Age, hemoglobin, platelets, blast percentage, cytogenetics
- Favorable to unfavorable prognosis classification
- Treatment approach guidance

### 15. IPSS-R Score (ipss-r-score.html)
**Purpose:** Revised International Prognostic Scoring System for MDS
**Features:**
- Cytopenia severity, blast percentage, ring sideroblasts, cytogenetics
- 5-tier risk stratification (Very Low to Very High)
- Median OS estimates and therapeutic recommendations

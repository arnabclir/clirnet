7. POST-TEST PROBABILITY FROM LIKELIHOOD RATIOS
   File: /home/user/clirnet/calculators/calculators/post-test-probability.html
   Size: 19 KB
   
   Purpose: Bayesian calculation of disease probability after testing
   Inputs: Pre-test probability (%), LR+, LR-
   Outputs:
   • Post-test probability after positive result
   • Post-test probability after negative result
   • Visual probability bars
   Formula: Bayesian calculation using odds
   1. Pre-test odds = P / (1-P)
   2. Post-test odds = Pre-test odds × LR
   3. Post-test prob = Odds / (1 + Odds)
   Clinical Features:
   - Pre-test probability input (0-100%)
   - Separate LR+ and LR- inputs
   - Color-coded visual representation
   - Interpretation guidance for each scenario
   - LR interpretation scale (>10, 5-10, 2-5, etc.)
   - Evidence-based diagnostic guidance
   - Decision support for treat/test/reassure
   - Comprehensive formula section
